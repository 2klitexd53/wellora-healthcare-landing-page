## 🔹 FAQ SECTION

### Section Label
*Common Questions*

### Headline
**Got Questions? We've Got Answers.**

---

**Q: Is NourishCare a fitness app?**
No. NourishCare is purely focused on nutrition and health management, not workouts or exercise tracking. It's built around what you eat, how you're feeling, and keeping your health routine on track.

**Q: Can I use NourishCare to manage someone else's health?**
Yes. Caregivers can connect to a family member or patient's profile and stay updated on their nutrition logs, medication schedule, and health feedback, with appropriate consent.

**Q: Do I need to be tech-savvy to use it?**
Not at all. NourishCare is designed from the ground up to be simple and clear for users of all ages, including seniors who may not use apps regularly.

**Q: How does the doctor access feature work?**
NourishCare helps you quickly connect with healthcare providers through the app, reducing the friction of scheduling or waiting to get straightforward answers about your health.

**Q: Is my health data secure?**
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and data handling practices. Your information is never sold or shared without your explicit consent.
